Introduction

Featured PROJECTS THAT WE LOVE.

Easy to Use

Compared with normally complicated and heavy passenger screening project, we want to build up a much simpler one which is small and exquisite. With our project, we can ease the situation of long waiting lane in TSA and some other public places with passenger screening.

More Accuracy

Although we want to make an agility program, we still want it to maintain a high level of accuracy. After all our project has an accuracy over 90%.

More Efficient

Since our project is small and exquisite, then it will have the advantage that it will be much more efficient than traditional passenger project. Once you upload the correct type of file, our program will immediately tell you whether there is any threat or not.
